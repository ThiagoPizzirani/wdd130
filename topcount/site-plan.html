WDD 130 - Section 8

Overview

Purpose

Create a website to promote and increase awareness of Accounting services for business owners. It should explain the benefits of the services and why our company could be a great solution with a visual language that builds trust and confidence. The website will include a simulated functionality to book a consultation with a specialist.

Audience

Small business owners in the U.S. that do not have their own accountant or don't know how to manage their own accounting books themselves.

Hero Area: Are you a small business owner looking to book a call with an accountant specialist? With so many options available, it can be difficult to determine which one is best for your business.
